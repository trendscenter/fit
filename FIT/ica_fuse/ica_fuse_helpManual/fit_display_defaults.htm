Display Defaults

The controls in the "Display Defaults" menu are explained as follows:

Convert To Z-scores
Converts spatial maps to z-scores.
Threshold Value
This is the Z threshold value.
Image Values
There are four options like 'Positive', 'Positive and Negative', 'Absolute', 'Negative'. 'Positive' and 'Negative' refers to activations and de-activations on spatial map.
Images Per Figure
Number of images per figure. Options are '1', '4' and '9'.
Anatomical Plane
Options are 'Axial', 'Sagittal' or 'Coronal'.
Slices Range
Slices plotted in mm.
Note: Display defaults are defined in 'ica_fuse_defaults.m'.
